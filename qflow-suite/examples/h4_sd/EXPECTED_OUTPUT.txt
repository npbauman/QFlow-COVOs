Expected output for the bundled H4/STO-3G example (linear H4 chain,
bond lengths 1.0 Angstrom, generated with scripts/generate_h4_example.py).

This is a minimal but nontrivial active-space test case:
  noas = nobs = 2 (occupied spin-orbitals per spin channel)
  nvas = nvbs = 2 (virtual spin-orbitals per spin channel)
  nactives = comb(2,2) * comb(2,2) = 1  (exactly one active space)

Because there is only one active space and it spans the entire
virtual/occupied space (no "rest of system" left over to mark
inactive), this example is primarily a connectivity/smoke test for
the covo-ci -> qflow-suite pipeline, not a demonstration of QFlow's
multi-active-space iterative refinement (which requires larger
systems with several active spaces). It is, however, a genuine and
useful correctness check: SDTQ should recover the FCI energy almost
exactly, since with all four excitation ranks included and only one
active space, there is no remaining truncation error.

Step 1: covo-ci FCI reference (Full CI, exact for this system)
----------------------------------------------------------------
  covo-ci run --mode aperiodic --input-dir . --excitation-level full \
              --n-roots 1 --save-matrix FCI_matrix.dat

  state 1: E = -2.1663874486   multiplicity = 1

Step 2: QFlow-SD (singles + doubles only)
----------------------------------------------------------------
  qflow-suite run --level sd --n-elec 4 --norb 4 \
              --matrix-file FCI_matrix.dat --actspin-file nactspin_fortran.dat \
              --actspin-columns 8

  Final QFlow-SD energy: approximately -2.1663060 (converged after 20
  global iterations). This is variationally above the exact FCI
  energy, as expected for an SD-truncated method -- the residual
  ~8e-5 Hartree gap corresponds to triples/quadruples correlation
  energy not captured at this truncation level.

Step 3: QFlow-SDTQ (singles, doubles, triples, quadruples)
----------------------------------------------------------------
  qflow-suite run --level sdtq --n-elec 4 --norb 4 \
              --matrix-file FCI_matrix.dat --actspin-file nactspin_fortran.dat

  Final QFlow-SDTQ energy: approximately -2.1663874 (converged after
  20 global iterations), matching the exact FCI energy to ~5e-8
  Hartree -- confirming both the covo-ci matrix and the QFlow-SDTQ
  wrapper are working correctly together.

Note: exact converged values may vary in the last 1-2 reported digits
depending on numpy/scipy/numba versions and BLAS backend; the
important checks are (1) QFlow-SD lands close to but above the FCI
energy, and (2) QFlow-SDTQ converges much closer to the FCI energy
than QFlow-SD does.
